  • Greetings

In this lesson, you will learn how to greet people in Swahili.

Hello!
Jambo!

This is the most common way to greet a Swahili speaker.

Hello, how are you?
Hujambo?

This is the most common way to respond to "Hujambo?".

Hello, I am fine.
Sijambo.
Hello Anna, how are you?
Anna?
question
Thank you.
Asante.

In response to the greeting "Hujambo!", here is one way to politely respond.

I am fine, thank you.
Sijambo, asante.
How are you?
Habari gani?
