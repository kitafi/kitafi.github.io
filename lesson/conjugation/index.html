Swahili verbs often start with ku in the infinitive form.

to study
kusoma

To conjugate this verb in the present tense, the prefix ku is first dropped, and other prefixes are added to indicate who is performing the verb and when they are performing it. In this case, ni is the prefix for the first person (I), and na is the prefix for the present tense.

I am studying
ninasoma

test

You are studying
unasoma foo
