When referring to a plural number of items, the noun changes.

my teachers
walimu wangu

When you are part of a group that owns something, use etu.

our teacher
mwalimu wetu

When using certain family words like mothers (mama), fathers (baba), sisters (dada), and friends (marafiki) in the plural, the possessive word is zangu.

my .
zangu
