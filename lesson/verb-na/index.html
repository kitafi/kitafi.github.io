The verb "to have" is constructed by adding a prefix to na.

a friend.
rafiki .

When using "to have" in the past tense, the root na becomes likuwa na.

I had a car.
Nilikuwa na gari.

How would you conjugate the phrase "you had"?

You had a dog.
mbwa .

How would you conjugate the phrase "you all had"?

You all had a cat.
paka .

When using "to have" in the future tense, the root na becomes takuwa na.

We will have work.
Tutakuwa na kazi .

Translate the following sentence. Use the pronouns yeye and wao, and remember lakini is the word for "but".

She has a cat, but they will have a dog.
