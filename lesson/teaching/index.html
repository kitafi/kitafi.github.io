This word is also in the M-WA noun class.

student, students
mwanafunzi, wanafunzi

How would you politely greet a classroom of students?

Hello students, how are you?
wanafunzi?

How would the students politely respond to you?

Hello professor, we are good.
.

You might start a class by telling students what they will be learning today.

Today we will learn .
Leo tutasoma .

How would a student tell you they have a question?

I have a question.
swali.

How would you ask a particular student if they have a question?

Do you have a question?
?

How would you ask all the students if they have a question?

Do you have a question?
?
