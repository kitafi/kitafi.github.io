to say
kusema
I say

By adding je at the end of a conjugated verb, we are converting it to a question on how the particular action is performed.

How do I say ?
Ninasemaje ?

Conjugate the verb kuenda (to go) in the past tense.

you went

How do you think you would ask "how did you go"?

How did you go?
?

How do you think you would ask "how will you go"? Remember to conjugate kuenda (to go) in the future tense.

How will you go to the store?
dukani ?
