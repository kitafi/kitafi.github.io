How would you conjugate kutoka (to come from) in the first person?

I come from America.
Mimi nchi ya Marekani.

The verb kukaa means "to live" or "to reside". The verb kuishi means "to live", "to exist", or "to remain". Both can be used when asking someone where they currently live.

Where do you live?
Unakaa/Unaishi wapi
