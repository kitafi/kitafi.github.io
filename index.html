Free online lessons to learn Swahili.

Swahili is a Bantu language that is widely spoken in East Africa. This collection of interactive lessons and quizzes will teach you everything from vocabulary to grammar, so you can speak Swahili with confidence and accuracy.

All of the content is free and open-source, so anyone can create new lessons and easily repurpose the content on this website. Click to learn more about the MIT license.
